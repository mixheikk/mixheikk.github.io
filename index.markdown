My research is mostly focused on trustworthy machine learning, especially on privacy-preserving (differentially private) machine learning. I'm also interested in machine learning more generally, and have worked plenty, e.g., on learning from distributed data (federated learning), as well as Bayesian learning. I received my PhD in 2023 from the Department of Statistics, University of Helsinki, Finland, working in the group lead by Prof. Antti Honkela.
